
## 🌐 Domain Appraisal

**Domain appraisals** are assessments that determine the **market value of a domain name**. These are essential for both buyers and sellers to understand the worth of a domain before making a transaction. Below is a complete overview with examples.

---

## 📘 What is Domain Appraisal?

A domain appraisal evaluates several factors to estimate the **monetary value** of a domain name. Key factors include:

* **Length and memorability**: Short, catchy names are generally more valuable.
* **Keyword popularity**: Domains with popular keywords tend to attract more traffic.
* **Search Engine Optimization (SEO)**: SEO-friendly domains may be more desirable.
* **Market trends**: The current demand for certain domain types affects their value.
* **Comparable sales data**: Historical sales of similar domains serve as a benchmark.

Domain appraisals can be conducted by professionals or through automated tools provided by registrars and online services.

---

## 🛠 Examples of Domain Appraisal Services

1. **Sedo** – Offers professional domain valuation using scientific analysis, with reports delivered within 5 business days. Pricing starts at \$99.
