
## **The Story of DNS: The Beginning of a Journey**

**Characters**:

* **Nikhil**: A curious child who loves exploring new things on the internet.
* **Browser**: Nikhil's friend who helps him reach websites.
* **DNS Resolver**: A wise guide who shows Nikhil the right path.
* **Root Server**: A high-level guide who stands at the top.
* **TLD Server**: A gatekeeper who leads in the right direction.
* **Authoritative Name Server**: A knowledgeable figure who provides accurate information.

---

### **The Beginning of the Story**

One day, Nikhil said to his browser, “Let’s go to `www.wikipedia.org` today!” He typed it in, and his journey began.

**Browser** said, “Alright, Nikhil! But I need the IP address for that. Let’s ask the DNS Resolver.”

---

### **Step 1: Asking the DNS Resolver for Help**

The **browser** asked the DNS Resolver, “Do you know the IP address of `www.wikipedia.org`?”
The **DNS Resolver** replied, “Let me first check my cache. If I already know it, I’ll tell you right away.”

---

### **Step 2: Checking the Cache**

The **DNS Resolver** checked its cache, but didn’t find anything.
“I’ll have to search for it further,” he said.

---

### **Step 3: Journey to the Root Server**

The **DNS Resolver** headed to the Root Server.
“Hey Root Server, do you know where `www.wikipedia.org` is?”
The **Root Server** smiled and said, “Yes, I do! You need to go to the `.org` TLD Server.”

---

### **Step 4: The TLD Server’s Gate**

The **DNS Resolver** asked the TLD Server, “Can you tell me the name server for `www.wikipedia.org`?”
The **TLD Server** said, “Of course! Go to the authoritative name server.”

---

### **Step 5: Getting Info from the Authoritative Name Server**

The **DNS Resolver** asked the authoritative name server, “Can you tell me the IP address of `www.wikipedia.org`?”
The **Authoritative Name Server** said, “Certainly! It is 208.80.154.224.”

---

### **Step 6: Sending the Information Back**

The **DNS Resolver** sent this IP address back to the **browser**.
“Now I know it. Let’s connect!” said the **browser**.

---

### **Step 7: Connecting to the Website**

The **browser** used the IP address to connect to `www.wikipedia.org`.
The page loaded, and Nikhil entered a world full of knowledge and information.

---

### **Step 8: The Magic of Caching**

“Next time when I visit here, will I have to ask again?” Nikhil asked.
The **DNS Resolver** smiled and said, “No! I’ll cache it. Next time you’ll reach faster!”

---

### **Moral of the Story**

Thus, Nikhil’s journey showed how DNS works. From typing a simple name to getting the correct IP address and loading the website, DNS acts like a magical guide. It helps us smoothly navigate the internet, just like a good friend.
